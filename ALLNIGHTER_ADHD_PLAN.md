## ðŸ§˜ ADHD Coping Strategies

### When Stuck (>15 mins)
1. Switch to different feature
2. Take 5-min walk
3. Explain problem out loud
4. Ask AI for help
5. Document the blocker and move on

### When Overwhelmed
1. List 3 immediate next steps
2. Pick the easiest one
3. Set 15-min timer
4. Celebrate small win
5. Repeat

### When Losing Focus
1. Change podcast
2. Stand up and stretch
3. Drink water
4. Check progress tracker
5. Pick a "fun" task
