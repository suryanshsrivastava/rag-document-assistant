# Krazimo Take Home Assignment

## Overview

Welcome to our technical assessment\! This assignment is designed to evaluate your full-stack AI engineering capabilities through your progress, approach, and problem-solving methodology. We want to see how you think, plan, and execute on complex AI-powered applications.

**Time Allocation:** 2 days  
**Tech Stack Required:** Next.js, FastAPI, Supabase

**Expectation:** We want to see your progress and approach, not necessarily a fully polished product

---

## What We're Really Evaluating

**Your Development Process (40%)**

* How you break down complex problems into manageable pieces  
* Your prioritization of features and implementation order  
* Quality of your planning and architectural decisions  
* How you handle challenges and pivot when needed

**Technical Competency (35%)**

* Code quality and organization in what you do build  
* Understanding of AI concepts and implementation approaches  
* Effective use of the required tech stack  
* Problem-solving skills and technical reasoning

**Communication & Documentation (25%)**

* Ability to explain your thought process and decisions  
* Quality of documentation for what you've built  
* Clear articulation of next steps and remaining work  
* Honest assessment of progress and challenges

---

### **AI-Powered Development**

Use AI tools to help you code\! This shows you're up-to-date with modern development.

**Recommended Tool**: Cursor IDE (or any AI-powered IDE you prefer)

### **Open Source Libraries**

Use any open-source libraries you want. We care more about *why* you chose them than *which* ones you picked.

## **Problem Statement**

### **Intelligent Document Workspace**

Create an AI-powered system where users can upload documents and have intelligent conversations about their content.

**Sample user journey:**

* Upload research papers, reports, or articles  
* Ask complex questions that span multiple documents  
* Get insights, summaries, and connections between documents  
* Build knowledge over time through accumulated interactions

---

**1\. Clear Problem Definition & Scope**

**Tell us:**

* What specific aspect you decided to focus on  
* What your target user experience looks like

## Technical Requirements

**Core Components to Address:**

1. **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**  
* Document ingestion and processing pipeline  
* Vector embedding and storage strategy  
* Semantic search and retrieval mechanism  
* Response generation with source attribution  
2. **AI Agent Capabilities**  
* Multi-step reasoning or conversation flow  
* Integration with external tools or APIs  
* Context management across interactions  
* Decision-making and response routing  
3. **Full Stack Implementation**  
* Frontend: User interface for your chosen problem domain  
* Backend: API endpoints supporting AI functionality  
* Database: Data persistence for users, documents, and interactions
